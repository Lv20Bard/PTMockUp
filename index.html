Facts about Point Tupper

  • Deep water, ice free and dredge free harbor

  • Capable of accommodating ultra large carriers (500,000 dwt)

  • Limiting depth of 92 feet (27 meters)

  • Currents: 0.5 knots - 1.5 knots

  • Sable Island - 117 nautical miles

  • An abundance of industrial zoned, underdeveloped land and property available for purchase

  • Home to the Nautical Institute, Nova Scotia Community College
